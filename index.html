The Internet of Things

Connecting the world, one device at a time.

The internet of things is a web of either physical objects or 'things' that have software or other technologies inside of them. They can exchange data with other devices through the internet without the need of any human action.

How IoT Works

IoT works by sending data to a server which it sends it to another server. For e.g. when opening a garage with your phone, you press a button which sends a signal toa server which sends it to the machine that opens the garage door.

Our Role in the IoT Revolution

The I-Vision will play a key role in this revolution with it's innovative features which allow you to access the internet via command by the head or by verbal command.
